Good Monday, friends of privacy!

Last week has been full on branches, exploits and leaks showcasing strongly the importance of solid and strong structures and power of good cryptography. It's precisely incidents like these that proves once again that we have to work hard for making privacy universal and strong.

Rather then falling into doom-and-gloom mindset, let's take a look together at Nox. A mixnet for EVM blockchains with anonymous transaction relay by team Hisoka, our Tool of the week.
