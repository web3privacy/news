Last couple of days were full of many important signals from goverments and it's on us, how we react. While it's great to see for example France opting out for more open-source tools and migrating their government computers to Linux, Zambia has managed to cancel anticipated RightsCon.

So in solidarity with the great people of RightsCon and AccessNow, tool of the week is their #WhyID campaign.
