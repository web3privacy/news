Good Monday, friends of privacy!

Another week is in the books, with many new updates and public pushes as well as digital rights progress. Be it implementation of privacy features in Big Tech hardware or new projects and tools in web3 realm, there is something for everyone. 

If you are in Cannes right now, enjoy the event. And if you have a time, take a look at new Ethereum rollup, Ethereum Economic Zone, our Tool of The week.

 Made by people from Gnosis and ZiskVM and co-founded by Ethereum Foundation, Ethereum Economic Zone is framework built around one principle: rollups that extend Ethereum, not branch away from it.
