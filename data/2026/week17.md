Good Monday, friends of privacy!

If you are living with internet connection, chance is you already know about many people's many attempts for control over data, privacy or human rights. And while it's important to keep on track of those and pay attention, we here feel it's equally important to cherish our accomplishments.

Whenever there is someone forcing down KYC or age restriction, let's celebrate another great project making it possible to exchange money without revealing anything that doesn't need to be revelead.

Peer lets you buy or sell crypto directly with another person without unneccesarrily revealing your data, basically feeling like sending money to your friend. And now it is also our Tool of the Week.
