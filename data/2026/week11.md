Good Monday, friends of privacy!

In the recent wave of people trying to change the tools and apps they are using to opt for more privacy-focused options, we think it is good to help and navigate a bit where to look. Lots of people are now cancelling their ChatGPT subscriptions and are on the lookout for alternative, so we think it's good time to take a look at Qwen.

Open-source LLM where you don't need to create an account. So take a look!
