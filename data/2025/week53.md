Good Monday, friends of privacy!

The year of 2025 is coming to an end and we here at Web3Privacy are busy as ever with all your submissions for Privacy Awards. While going through your inquiries for best and worse additions to privacy ecosystems, we couldn't miss how Zoom does it's behavorial surveillance and collects data about your clicking, typings and so on. But you know who doesn't? Brave Talk!

Brave's team are working everyday to enhance the world of privacy with their search, AI and even private Meeting Spaces. So give next time somebody invites you to some Zoom call, keep them in mind and give it a try.

This is gonna be the last newsletter of this year, but worry not, you will keep on hearing from us next year even more! See you around and wish you Happy New Year full of Privacy championing!
